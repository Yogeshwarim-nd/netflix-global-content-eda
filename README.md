# Netflix Global Content EDA

## Project Overview

This project performs **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on Netflix’s global content catalog to uncover trends in movies and TV shows.
The analysis aims to generate **data-driven insights** that can help Netflix make strategic decisions regarding content production, release timing, and regional content distribution.

---

## Problem Statement

The objective of this analysis is to explore Netflix’s content catalog and generate insights that can help the company make strategic decisions about:

* The type of movies and TV shows to produce
* Optimal release strategies
* Expanding subscriber growth across different countries

By analyzing historical content data, the project identifies patterns in content production, genre popularity, and global distribution.

---

## Dataset Information

The dataset contains information about Netflix titles, including:

* Title
* Genre
* Release Year
* Rating
* Duration
* Cast
* Director
* Country of Production

---

## Key Questions Explored

### 1. Content Popularity by Country

* Which types of content (Movies vs TV Shows) are most common in different countries?
* Are there specific genres that dominate certain regions?

### 2. Content Production Trends

* How has the production of movies and TV shows changed over time?

### 3. Influence of Cast and Crew

* Are specific actors, directors, or genres associated with higher content production?

### 4. Optimal Release Timing

* When is the ideal time to release a TV show to maximize audience engagement?

### 5. Content Focus in Recent Years

* Does Netflix focus more on **TV Shows or Movies** in recent years?

### 6. Global Content Distribution

* How is content distributed across different countries?
* What opportunities exist for growth in underrepresented regions?

## Key Insights

Some of the insights derived from the analysis include:

* Movies constitute a larger portion of Netflix’s catalog compared to TV Shows.
* Certain countries dominate content production on the platform.
* Content production increased significantly after the mid-2010s.
* Drama and Comedy are among the most frequently produced genres.
* Netflix has increased its focus on TV shows in recent years.
